Privacy Policy

Sudoku Edge
Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Sudoku Edge ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Local Data Only: All your game data is stored locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers.

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game statistics (games played, win rates, best times)
  • User preferences (username)
  • Game progress and scores

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect certain information to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by our advertising partners (Google AdMob) as described above.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is a puzzle game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require registration or personal information to play.

6. Data Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can reset all your game statistics at any time through the Profile screen in the app
  • You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
